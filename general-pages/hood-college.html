Hood College:
Site Redesign

Agency: OHO Interactive
Role: UX Designer, Visual Designer, Frontend Developer, QA
Software Used: Sketch, Illustrator, Brackets (HTML, CSS, JS), Drupal
Image Credit: Hood College

An Introduction

Hood College is an independent liberal arts college located on a striking and stunningly picturesque campus in the vibrant and historic central Maryland city of Frederick

OHO Interactive

I worked with OHO Interactive to bring out the vibrant community and beautiful campus Hood College had to offer. This went from their brightly colored cap tradition to every one of their student stories.

Image Credit: Hood College

Opportunities for Hood

During the project, I considered a variety of marketing strategies and UX best practices.

We wanted to give them more opportunities to show visual and written content, all without distracting the secondary audience (the current students) with recruitment and marketing.

The Application Process Page

One of the pages I worked on was their application process page. I wanted to break it down into simple steps, instead of their former text-dense pages. I incorporated suggested sections and a way to navigate through the sections in the page with a fixed navigation.

Not only does this help with user navigation, this component is useful for first-generation students that have limited college guidance.

Strategy

You can see other bits of strategy incorporated in their site. This goes from the process page to help bring in more applications and talented students, to the program pages highlighting student success, to serving internal audiences like the current students.
