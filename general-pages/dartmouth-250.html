Dartmouth College:
250th Website

Agency: OHO Interactive
Role: UX Designer, Information Architect
Software Used: Sketch

Overview

Dartmouth College reached out to OHO to work on a site to celebrate their 250th anniversary. The site was to feature various Dartmouth monuments, people, milestones, and future endeavors.

In this project, the site had two custom wireframes that had to be made, the homepage, and a timeline page, each having its own logistical challenges. I was the primary UX Designer for this project with guidance from my supervisor.

Guiding Questions

These were some of the questions or concerns we had in mind while redesigning their site.

  • How do we design the site to feature not only events from the past 250 years, but the goals they hope to obtain in the future?
  • How can we make the page interactive for the user and not just a giant listing of images and text?
  • How do we feature all aspects of Dartmouth without seemingly favoring one over another? (i.e., how can we prevent one Dartmouth internal team from being featured more than another?)
  • How can we use the site to lead users to the capital campaign site?
