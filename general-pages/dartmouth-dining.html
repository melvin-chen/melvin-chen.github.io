Dartmouth College:
Dining Sitemap

Agency: OHO Interactive
Role: Information Architect
Software Used: Sketch

An Introduction

Dartmouth College reached out to OHO to work on restructuring their dining site. With a smaller budget, Dartmouth wanted to focus on using their current web templates but structure their site in a way that would be easy to navigate.

What is Information Architecture (IA)?

IA is simply how parts of the page relate to each other in a way that is understandable to the user. It tells the UX designer and client how the site will be structure and what page templates will be needed to create the website.

In this project, we delivered a sitemap with recommendations for which pre-existing templates to use from their resources.

Their Current Site

After a thorough reading of their current site, we identified a few things they could improve on or highlight:

  • Reducing the amount of text
  • Provide information to those other than current students (e.g., parents, prospective students, etc.)
  • Show the options students with restrictive diets could have
  • Highlight "The Scoop", a service students can sign up that updates them promotions or closures
  • Highlight events Dartmouth Dining holds

The New Site Architecture

The proposed sitemap broke down the site into more inclusive sections. With the new sitemap, information is broken down into these sections:

  • Hours and Locations: for current students to get quick information about the hours
  • An Interstitial Menu page: They have separate site for their menu, but having an interstitial page before linking off creates a better user experience
  • Meal Plans: This section is for current and prospective students to learn about their options
  • Inclusive Dining: For everyone to learn about the services Dartmouth provides to include those with restrictive diets (e.g., Kosher/Halal dining, allergies, cultural dining, and vegetarian diets)
  • Events: A canonical source for students to find dining events
  • Initiatives: Dartmouth wanted to communicate the efforts they put for students, like their community engagement and sustainability
  • About Us: A simple blurb about the dining team
